Affiliate Program

About the Program

The TONBANKCARD affiliate program lets you earn TBC tokens on every swap you complete and on every swap made by users you refer. Points are awarded automatically when a swap reaches the finished state.

This page explains the exact formulas used to calculate your points, the applicable caps and vesting rules, and how to redeem points for TBC tokens.

Point Award Formula

Points awarded to a participant for a swap are calculated as:

points = floor( turnover_usd × bps / 10000 / 0.00003 )

At the current 0.10% cashback rate this equals roughly 33 points per $1 of swap turnover.

Redemption

Accumulated points can be redeemed for TBC tokens credited to your TONBANKCARD wallet.

  • Minimum redemption: 100 points (= 10 TBC) to keep the on-chain transfer cost below the redeemed value.
  • Formula: TBC = floor(points / 10); USD value = TBC × $0.0003.
  • TBC value is variable. We credit a fixed token amount, not a USD value.

Caps, Vesting, and Anti-Fraud Rules

  • Daily turnover cap on point awarding per user (configurable; default $50,000/day).
  • Telegram account age ≥ 7 days before earned points become withdrawable.
  • Accounts where more than 80% of referral volume comes from a single referee are flagged for review.
  • Self-referral and cyclic referral chains are rejected. Referral bonuses only pay after the referee's swap reaches finished state.

Disclosure

TBC is the native token of the TONBANKCARD ecosystem. Its market price is variable and not guaranteed. The program credits a fixed number of TBC tokens — not a fixed USD value.

All commission rates (cashback_bps, referral_bps, service_bps) are runtime-configurable. Any change takes effect only for swaps completed after the change; the historical ledger is immutable.
