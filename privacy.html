Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This notice explains how TON Bridge handles personal data when you use the Telegram Mini App and website.

Data We Process

TON Bridge is non-custodial and does not require account registration. Depending on your actions, we may process:

  • Technical data such as device, browser, language, theme, and Telegram Web App context needed to run the app.
  • Transaction-related details you enter or receive from exchange partners, such as wallet addresses, assets, amounts, and order status.
  • Consent preferences stored locally and, when available, mirrored to Telegram CloudStorage.
  • Analytics identifiers and session events only after you consent to analytics.

Third-Party Processors

We use third-party services only where needed to operate, measure, or complete exchanges:

  • Yandex.Metrika, including Webvisor, for product analytics after analytics consent.
  • Telegram Analytics for Telegram Mini App usage analytics after analytics consent.
  • ChangeNOW and related exchange infrastructure to process swaps and show order status.

Retention

Consent preferences are kept for up to 12 months. Transaction and support records are kept only as long as needed for service, dispute handling, legal obligations, and security.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent by contacting us. You can also clear local storage in your browser or Telegram Web App to reset consent.

GDPR Legal Basis

Necessary processing is based on contract performance and legitimate interests in operating a secure non-custodial service. Analytics and marketing processing are based on consent under GDPR Article 6(1)(a).

Russian Personal Data Law

Where Russian Federal Law No. 152-FZ applies, we process personal data for stated service purposes, apply minimization, and use consent where required.

Contact
